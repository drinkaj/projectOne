The Sport of Motocross

Motocross is an off-road motorcycle racing sport where riders compete on closed circuits featuring natural terrain, dirt tracks, steep jumps, and sharp corners. The term is a portmanteau of the French word motocyclette (motorcycle) and cross-country. It is widely recognized as one of the most physically demanding sports in the world.
